   This dashboard helps you monitor sales performance and customer behavior across Blinkit outlets. You use it to identify high-performing outlets, top-selling categories, and demand patterns by location and size.

**5. Features / Highlights**

**‚Ä¢ Business Problem**
Blinkit operates across multiple outlet types and city tiers. Business teams need a clear way to track performance differences by location, outlet size, and product category. Raw tables do not show trends, demand concentration, or underperforming segments clearly.

Key questions include:

Which outlet tier generates the highest sales?

Which product categories drive volume?

How does outlet size impact revenue?

Are customer ratings consistent across outlet types?

**‚Ä¢ Goal of the Dashboard**

To provide an interactive performance view that:
Enables quick comparison of outlets by tier, size, and type
Highlights top and low-performing product categories
Supports inventory planning and outlet expansion decisions
Tracks long-term sales trends by outlet establishment year.

**‚Ä¢ Business Impact & Insights**

**Inventory Optimization:** You can prioritize high-demand categories like fruits, snacks, and household items.

**Expansion Strategy:** Tier 3 cities show the strongest revenue potential for new outlets.

**Outlet Planning:** Medium-sized outlets deliver the best sales performance.

**Performance Monitoring:** Consistent ratings across outlet types indicate stable customer satisfaction.
